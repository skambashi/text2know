Text2Know

1. You text a command

There are various commands such as 'Google Maps' or 'Weather'. Just text us 'cmds' to get a list of working commands.

2. We process your command

After receiving and processing your message, we fetch your data from the interwebz.

3. We text a response

Once the info has been grabbed, we text it back to you.
